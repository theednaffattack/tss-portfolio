- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (full fat is best for thickness)
- 2-3 garlic cloves, minced to a paste
- Juice of 1/2 lemon (or 1-2 tbsp lemon juice)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh dill or parsley (finely chopped, optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine the yogurt, minced garlic, and lemon juice in a small bowl.
1. Add olive oil and fresh herbs if using, and stir well until fully incorporated.
1. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
1. For best results, cover and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to blend.
